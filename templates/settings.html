📋 How to Customise

Accent Colour — The vertical bar on the left of each embed. Pick any hex colour.

Footer Text — Small text at the bottom of every embed. Great for server name or tagline.

Thumbnail — Image in the top-right corner of embeds. Use your server logo.

Author — Line shown above the embed title. Good for branding.
